Chocolate Chip Muffin

Description

A very simple and easy recipe for tasty chocolate chip muffins. Quickly make a dozen muffins on your table.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Grease bottom of 12 muffin cups or line with baking cups.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix flour, 1/2 cup of sugar, baking powder, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a small bowl, mix milk, oil and eggs.
  4. Combine all ingredients at once; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. Sprinkle tops of muffins before baking with a combination of 3 tablespoons sugar and 2 tablespoon brown sugar.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until toothinpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Cool for 1 minute before removing from pan.
